Serve IT
Serve IT applies technology skills in order to build the capacity of local nonprofits so they may better serve their own missions.
Boys and Girls Club Btown Community Orchard
Boys and Girls Club
Mission Statement
"To empower all young people, especially those who need us most, to reach their full potential as caring, productive, and responsible citizens."
Problem Statement
Teach-IT hosts a weekly club for students at the Crestmont Boys and Girls club. This club focuses on teaching students about technology and other STEM topics.
Team Mission
Teach-IT gives presentations every week about topics such as: probability, scratch coding and computer art. These presentations have been created by past and current IU students. As a Teach-IT intern I worked to inspire students and engage them in learning by teaching hands on STEM activities. These activities included educational games, Scratch coding and fun quizzes.
The Work
Our team worked with Crestmont Boys and Girls club in Bloomington. We used Microsoft Teams to plan and develop in depth activities for the club each week. We met once a week for an hour and gave presentations on topics in STEM. These topics included probability, coding and online safety.
We came up with new lesson plans for future Teach IT teams to use. We worked with industry mentors to learn more about the field. We kept track of our hours on spread sheets and made sure we worked efficiently with our time.
Reflection
Our team did a good job at keeping students engaged and excited to participate in class. We taught the students more about the digital world and even learned some new things ourselves. It taught us more about using websites and platforms to work with a larger team. My favorite part of working at Teach IT was seeing the students who chose to come back every week. I was proud to know Teach IT was making a difference in their week. The experience has shown me the value of serving the community first hand.
