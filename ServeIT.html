Serve IT
Serve IT applies technology skills in order to build the capacity of local nonprofits so they may better serve their own missions.
Boys and Girls Club Btown Community Orchard
Boys and Girls Club
Mission Statement
"To empower all young people, especially those who need us most, to reach their full potential as caring, productive, and responsible citizens."
Problem Statement
Teach-IT hosts a weekly club for students at the Crestmont Boys and Girls club. This club focuses on teaching students about technology and other STEM topics.
Team Mission
Teach-IT gives presentations every week about topics such as: propability, scratch coding and computer art. These presentations have been created by past and current students. As Teach-IT interns we look to inspire students and engage them in learn by having hands on activities. These activities include games, scratch and fun quizzes.
The Work
Our team worked with Crestmont Boys and Girls club in Bloomington. We used Microsoft Teams to plan and develope in depth activities for the club each week. We met once a week for an hour and gave a presentation on a topic in STEM. These topics included: probability, coding and online safety.
We came up with new lesson plans for future Teach IT teams to use. We worked with industry mentors to learn more about the feild. We kept track of our hours on spread sheets and made sure we worked efficiently with our time.
Reflection
Our team has done a good job at keeping students engaged. Every week we saw new students excited to participate. We taught the students more about the digital world and even learned some new things for ourselves. It taught me more about using websites and platforms to work with a larger team. My favorite part of working at Teach IT was seeing the students who chose to come back every week. I knew Teach IT was making a difference in their week. The experience has shown me the value of serving the community first hand.
Accessibility for the Bloomington Orchard
Mission Statement
"To improve access to healthy, locally grown, and sustainable food in Monroe County by assessing the food system, educating the community on food issues, and advocating for policies that help support a stronger and healthier food system."
Problem Statement
The accessibility team works to provide constitency and access to those who need it most.
Team Mission
The Accessibility Team works on behalf of the Serve IT Clinic to check sites for usability and accessibility, ensuring that each site is the best it possibly can be. This work involves conducting various tests and assessments to help build sites.
The Work
At Serve IT I have worked with a team of several other hard working members of the community. We used several different tools such as Kanban, Trello and Microsoft Teams to communicate and work on several tasks at once. The work was meaningful and required lots of care and attention.
This work included using Axe Devtools and Google Web Developer to manage websites and check for accessibility requirements.
